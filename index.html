Welcome

I am a Bayesian Statistician and Data Scientist interested in studying real-life problems using data analytics and statistical methods, with experience working in the public, private, and consultancy sectors.

I work as Sr Expert Data Scientist at BBVA AI Factory Mexico unit, where I lead and develop advanced analytics solutions at large scale in private banking, payment systems, customer services, and generative AI. I am also Adjunt Professor at the Monterrey Tec Business School.

I hold a bachelor's degree in Actuarial Science from ITAM and earned a Master's degree in Statistics from UNAM under Professor Eduardo Gutiérez-Peña. My doctoral degree was completed at SMSAS University of Kent supervised by Professor Stephen G. Walker.

I am interested in the following academic research topics: Bayesian statistics, deep-learning methods, edge analytics, nonparametric methods of inference, prediction of complex dynamic systems, probabilistic graphical models, natural language processing, semantic data modeling, and behavioral analytics.
