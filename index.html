Welcome

I am a full-time professor at the Department of Actuarial Science and Insurance at ITAM. Here are the links to my [official website] and to my [professional profile].

My reserah interests are Bayesian paradigm to inference involving nonparametric methods, time-series analysis, highly-structured data, actuarial statistics, operational risks, quantitative risk assessment, macroeconometrics and predictive analytics. I am also interested in topics related to the visualization of complex data and the scalability of stochastic computational procedures.

I've worked in I received a bachellor degree in Actuarial Science from ITAM and a masters degree in Statistics at UNAM under Professor Eduardo Gutiérez-Pena. I was granted a doctoral degree at the University of Kent under supervision of Professor Stephen G. Walker. My masters degree was honoured with the Franciso Aranda-Ordaz Prize, and my doctoral dissertation received the Leonard J. Savage Award in the Theory and Methods cathegory.
